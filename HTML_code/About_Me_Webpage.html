My personal interest

One of my favorite interests is Star Wars, and not limited to the movies. It's a dead giveaway that I enjoy this series since one of my favorite books is Heir to the Empire by Timothy Zahn. I enjoy the lore and world and keep up with new shows, movies, and games whenever they come out. The movies and shows are pretty good for the most part, and I enjoy the world and design of the ships, plannets, and aliens.

Another big interest of mine is Dungeons and Dragons. I have played it since high school and have continued to play it through college. The biggest thing I like about it is the way you can tell stories with your friends in a collaborative way. I have played as a Dungeon Master and a player, and have had a lot of fun as both roles. I prefer being a Dungeon Master since I like to build the story around the characters, and make their actions have consequences.

I am really big into video games, it's my favorite pass time. Some of my favorite game series include Half-Life & Portal, Frostpunk, and Red Dead Redemption. I play a variety of other games, and it changes every so often, but I mainly enjoy the story and world of the games I play. I also enjoy a good challenge of strategy and tactics in games, and I enjoy good horror games like Phasmophobia, Dead Space, and Fears to Fathom.
